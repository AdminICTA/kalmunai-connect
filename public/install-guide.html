Kalmunai Connect - Installation Guide

This guide explains how to install Kalmunai Connect as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your device. Installing the app will allow you to access it directly from your home screen without opening a browser.

Benefits of installing the app:
  • Faster access from your home screen
  • Works offline or with poor internet connections
  • Receives important notifications
  • Uses less data than regular browsing

Install on Android

1 Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Chrome browser.
2 Tap the menu button (three dots) in the top-right corner.
3 Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" from the menu.
4 Tap "Install" in the dialog box that appears.
5 The app will be installed and an icon will appear on your home screen.

Install on iPhone/iPad

1 Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Safari browser.
2 Tap the Share button (rectangle with arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
3 Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen".
4 Tap "Add" in the upper-right corner.
5 The app will be installed and an icon will appear on your home screen.

Install on Desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux)

1 Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Chrome, Edge, or another compatible browser.
2 Look for the install icon (+ sign) in the address bar or the menu.
3 Click "Install" or "Install Kalmunai Connect".
4 The app will open in its own window and be added to your start menu or applications.

Troubleshooting

If you don't see the install option:

  • Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
  • Make sure you have a good internet connection for the first installation
