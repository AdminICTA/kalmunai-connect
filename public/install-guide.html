Kalmunai Connect Installation Guide

This guide will help you install the Kalmunai Connect application on your device for offline access and improved performance.

Installing on Android

  1. Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Chrome browser.
  2. Tap the three dots menu (â‹®) in the top-right corner.
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation.
  5. The app will now appear on your home screen.

Installing on iPhone/iPad

  1. Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Safari browser.
  2. Tap the Share button (rectangle with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Name the app and tap "Add".
  5. The app will now appear on your home screen.

Installing on Windows/Mac (Chrome, Edge)

  1. Open the Kalmunai Connect website in Chrome or Edge browser.
  2. Look for the install icon (+ in a box) in the address bar.
  3. Click on it and select "Install".
  4. If you don't see the install icon, click the three dots menu and select "Install Kalmunai Connect".
  5. The app will open in a separate window and be added to your Start menu or Applications folder.

Benefits of Installing the App

  • Faster access - no need to open a browser and type the URL
  • Works offline or with poor internet connections
  • Receives important notifications
  • Provides a more app-like experience
  • Uses less data than constantly loading the website
